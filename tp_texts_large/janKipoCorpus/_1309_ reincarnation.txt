The human body is a tool.  When it turns bad, the human brain just looks and continues.
The human brain is a tool.  When it turns bad, zurzgyn just looks and continues.
Zurzgyn (or Kalyndor) is inside your head and next to your brain and looks at your thoughts and so is amused.
Aydurkeni watches Zurzgyn.
The bodhisattva watches Aydurkheni.
Shiva can watch the bodhisattva.  But he doesn't look.  Shiva see everything in sleep.  This everything can exist.
Don't awaken Shiva!  That can be a disaster!
2

When the human brain dies, Zurzgyn becomes Kalyndor and discard the human brain.
Kalyndor gets into the fiery chariot and is next to Aydurkheni.  Aydurkheni drives this fiery chariot.
They go to the pleasant land in this chariot.
Again, they come from the pleasant land. This is not fun.
If the people of India think with their brains, they think that a new person has been born.


Sergey Tihonov
