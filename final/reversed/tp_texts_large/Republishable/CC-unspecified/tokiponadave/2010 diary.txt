 kin luka len e len mi

 mute len e len mi la mi tomo tan weka mi

 ona tomo insa lon awen li mute sike tenpo pi jan

 tu noka ilo kepeken kiwen lete telo lon tawa li lili jan

 kiwen li telo

 lon li mute lete

 2010 '' January `` 23 suno tenpo

 sina sijelo e lili ken li telo

 sijelo ike e pini ken li telo
